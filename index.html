Title2 - Commercial Title

SmartSteel

Project Description

The SmartSteel system is designed to enhance the efficiency and accuracy of inventory and order management for small and micro stainless steel businesses. The project is inspired by the need to transition from handwritten invoices to a digital, automated system, improving data visualization and reducing human errors. This system is particularly tailored for business owners, like my parents, who require a simple, user-friendly interface to manage their inventory, sales, customer records, and employee attendance effectively.
